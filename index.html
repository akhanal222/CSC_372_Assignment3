Dine On Campus

Welcome to the guide to find the best food spot within walking distance from the petty building. In this blog, I will give guidance to three different restaurants near the Petty Building that are affordable and delicious. The three restaurants I provide are among my favorite restaurants, and I will also provide must-try dishes from each restaurant. I've tried these spots myself and can say they never disappoint when it comes to great food close to campus.

Restaurant Near Petty Building

Crazy Mexico #5

Address: 332 Tate St, Greensboro, NC 27403

Phone: (336) 500-8890

Opening Hours : 6 AM - 10 PM

Service: Fast takeout, cheap prices for students, and big portions that fill you up.

Lunch Fajita Quesadilla

Flour tortillas filled with grilled steak or chicken and cheese, mushrooms, onions and bell peppers. Served with Spanish rice and sour cream salad.

Tacos de Pescado

Three flour tortilla tacos filled with grilled seasoned slices of tilapia. Served with lettuce, avocados, pico de gallo and Spanish rice.

Crazy Burrito

One burrito, chicken or steak, rice, beans, enchilada sauce and cheese dip.

Crazy Mexico is perfect when you're hungry but short on time. The tacos and burritos are filling, and the prices are student-friendly. I've found it's usually busiest at lunchtime, so going a little earlier or later helps beat the rush.

Tate Street Coffee House

Address: 334 Tate St, Greensboro, NC 27403

Phone: (336) 275-2754

Hours: 6:30 AM - 6 PM

Service: Outdoor seating, Free Wi-Fi, Fresh pastries

Breakfast Sandwich

Bagel or bread, egg, cheese, possibly bacon or sausage or ham and greens or tomato.

Cherry Danish

Pastry dough, cherry filling, possibly coated with glaze or sugar.

Little Italy

Espresso, steamed milk, flavoring, foam.

Tate Street Coffee House is a great place to relax and study with a cup of coffee. They've got outdoor seating, strong Wi-Fi, and fresh pastries that are perfect with a latte. It's usually packed in the mornings, but afternoons are more chill if you want a quieter vibe.

Sushi Republic

329 Tate St, Greensboro, NC 27403

Opening Hours: 11:30 AM - 2:30 PM, 5:00 - 9:30 PM

Phone: (336) 274-6684

Service : Outdoor seating, Great cocktails, Vegan options

Spicy Seafood Champon Ramen

A hearty noodle soup with shrimp, calamari, veggies, and ramen noodles in a rich, spicy broth.

Shrimp Tempura Roll

Crispy fried shrimp with avocado, cucumber, and a drizzle of eel sauce, crunchy on the outside, soft inside

Bibimbap

Rice bowl topped with saut√©ed vegetables, fried egg, and your choice of beef or tofu, served with spicy gochujang sauce on the side

Sushi Republic is a solid choice for dinner or a night out with friends. The sushi rolls are fresh, the ramen is comforting, and they've got vegan options too. It's busier in the evenings, so reservations or early dinners can make things easier.
